### No-Mess Microwave Oatmeal  
**Type:** Breakfast  
**Utility Tags:** #under5 #toddler #minimalmess #microwave  
**Flavor Tags:** #sweet #creamy (modular)  
**Toddler Rating:** #T-5 (if sweetened or topped)  

---

**Base Ingredients:**  
- ½ cup rolled oats  
- 1 cup water **or** milk (or ¾ cup water + ¼ cup milk)  
- Pinch of salt  
- Optional: ½ tbsp butter or 1 tbsp peanut butter

---

**Toppings (Mix & Match):**  
- Sliced banana, frozen berries, or diced apples  
- Brown sugar, maple syrup, honey  
- Cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla extract  
- Yogurt or cream to finish  
- Crushed nuts or granola (for adults)

---

**Instructions:**  
1. **Use a big bowl or oversized mug** — at least 4-cup capacity.  
2. Add oats, liquid, salt, and butter or PB if using. Stir.  
3. Microwave at **70% power for 2 minutes**.  
4. Stir gently. Continue in **30-second bursts**, watching closely, until texture is soft (~1–2 minutes more).  
5. Top and serve.

---

**Tips:**  
- Butter or peanut butter helps prevent boil-over  
- Stirring once midway helps prevent foaming  
- Add milk or yogurt *after* cooking for creaminess  
- Works well for “overnight oats” base too (just reheat next day)

---

**Storage:**  
- Can be pre-measured into mason jars or containers as a DIY instant oatmeal kit  
- Cooked oatmeal keeps 3–4 days in fridge  
- Reheat with a splash of milk or water

---

**Notes:**  
- Toddler-safe with soft fruit or mild flavor  
- Great fallback breakfast when energy is low
